What We Detect Today

This are the insights our platform extracts from natural city movement.

Potholes & Road Damage

We identify road cracks, potholes, and rough surfaces automatically as vehicles travel normally.

Driver Behavior Patterns

Overspeeding, harsh braking, reckless driving, and unusual patterns are mapped and alerted.

Bus & Fleet Movement

Live route adherence, delays, unexpected stops, congestion zones, and fleet reliability insights.

Mobility Flow Density

Heatmaps showing where people travel most, bottleneck routes, and under-served regions.

Environmental Exposure

Pollution hotspots identified through movement corridors and repeated travel patterns.

Citizen Road Reviews

Public ratings on road quality, safety, and comfort integrated into each mapped segment.

How We Detect

1

Natural Movement

Vehicles already moving generate organic data points across roads, buses, and highways.

2

Event Recognition

The system identifies meaningful patterns such as damage, behavior, congestion, or anomalies.

3

Geo-Tagging

Events are linked to GPS coordinates, severity scored, and verified with confidence levels.

4

Insights & Maps

Governments and operators view real-time dashboards that show actionable insights visually.
